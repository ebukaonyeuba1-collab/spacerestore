Who We Are

Premium Cleaning, Delta State Proud

SpaceRestore Cleaning Services was founded right here in Warri, Delta State. We started with a simple belief — that every Nigerian home and business deserves a truly clean, restored space.

We built a cleaning service that operates with the professionalism, reliability and care that clients in Delta State had always deserved. Today, we serve clients across Warri, Asaba, Sapele, Effurun, Ughelli and beyond.
