Our Story

Built on Pride. Delivered in Detail.

SpaceRestore was founded with one conviction: that the people of Delta State deserve a cleaning service that matches their ambition — professional, reliable, and exceptional.

The Beginning

Why SpaceRestore Exists

We started in Warri with a simple belief: that a clean, well-maintained space is not a luxury — it’s a foundation. For productivity, for health, for dignity.

We saw too many cleaning providers treat clients as afterthoughts. Unreliable. Untrained. Unprofessional. We decided to be different.

Today we operate across Delta State with a growing team of trained, uniformed professionals who take genuine pride in the work. Every clean is a SpaceRestore standard.

“We don’t just clean. We restore spaces and the people in them.”
